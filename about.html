About Memorabilia

What is Memorabilia?

For the sake of the deep love of technology that we've cultivated in these last 4 years and being crazy engineers at heart, we've concocted this simple website to keep our memories stored securely on the eternity of internet. So, go crazy with your memories, write some notes, posts some pics and upload some videos. Sign in with your name or be anonymous. The world is your oyster.

Why Memorabilia?

Because we've met some great people we never want to forget about because we did some crazy stuff that makes us feel happy whenever we remember them because we loved someone and didn't tell them enough because we missed out on some stuff we regret a lot about because we don't want to forget because it was our time, the Best Time of Your Life.

Dear friends, I think it is much clear now why we've started this. Go crazy with the platform, post as many pictures/videos/texts you like. Also write to us if you want to share something with the developers. And let us know if you'd like to see a music video made up from the photos posted here. Also if you'd like to contribute to the web development, checkout our github repo here.
